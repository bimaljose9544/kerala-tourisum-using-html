Benne Masala Dosa

Unlike the thin-crust dosas of Tamil Nadu, Benne Masala Dosa, is a crispy, golden crepe stuffed with a spiced potato filling. Soft on the inside and is cooked with plenty of butter to achieve that golden-brown crunchiness on the outside. It’s usually served with coconut chutney and a big dollop of butter to enhance the flavour.
