Munnar
A beautiful hill station known for tea plantations, misty hills, and pleasant climate. Popular for trekking and nature walks.
Alleppey (Alappuzha)
Famous for backwaters and houseboat cruises. Known as the "Venice of the East."
Thekkady
Home to Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. Great for wildlife safaris, spice plantations, and boat rides.
Kochi (Cochin)
A historical port city with colonial architecture, Chinese fishing nets, and the famous Fort Kochi area.
Wayanad
A scenic district with waterfalls, caves, wildlife, and spice plantations. Ideal for nature lovers and trekkers.
Kovalam
A beach town known for its crescent-shaped beaches, lighthouses, and Ayurvedic resorts.
Varkala
A unique beach with cliffs overlooking the Arabian Sea. Also famous for the Papanasam beach and Janardhana Swamy Temple.
Kumarakom
A peaceful village on the Vembanad Lake, ideal for backwater cruises, bird watching, and houseboat stays.
Athirappilly Waterfalls
Known as the "Niagara of India," this is the largest waterfall in Kerala, surrounded by lush green forest.
Bekal,
A small town known for the historic Bekal Fort and serene beaches. A quiet and scenic destination in north Kerala.
